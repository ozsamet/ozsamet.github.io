About

I'm Samet — a METU Statistics graduate working in actuarial oversight, the 2nd line of defence, across Sabancı Group's insurance entities (AgeSA, Aksigorta, Medisa).

That training gave me the quantitative foundation I rely on daily: probability and stochastic modelling, regression and time-series analysis, and the discipline to question a number before accepting it — exactly what independent reserve review demands.

Today I review and challenge reserving outputs for P&C and L&H portfolios, run sensitivity and variance analyses, and perform independent IBNR reasonableness checks across group entities — while automating the data validation and reconciliation work that audit-quality actuarial analysis depends on.

Before reserving, I spent a year as a data science intern at Allianz Türkiye, building predictive models and monitoring tools for motor pricing. I'm interested in where statistical rigour meets actuarial judgement: reserve risk, IFRS 17, and model validation.
